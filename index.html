Minjeong Park

I am working in the Vision & AI Lab at Korea University. Previously, I worked at Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) and Image and Video Pattern Recognition Lab as a student researcher. I interned in the Visual Media Lab at Kyunghee University and Laon People. I received my M.S in School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University. and B.S in Computer Science and Engineering Department at Kyunghee University.

My research interests lie in the intersection field of computer vision, especially on the tasks of multimodal representation learning, simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM), person retrieval and pedestrian attribute recognition.
