I am working in the Vision & AI Lab at Korea University. Previously, I worked at the Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) as a student researcher and Image and Video Pattern Recognition Lab at Yonsei University as a graduate research assistant.

My interests lie in the field of computer vision, machine learning, and its applications. Most recently, my research focuses on two main directions: (1) Ensuring robustness to distribution changes and unknown classes for real-world scenarios and (2) Developing an innovative vision algorithm to address diverse challenges across the representations in 2D and 3D.
